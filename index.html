What Is GDSC !!

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome .

GDSC stands for Google Developer's Student Club. It is a strong community lead by Google Developers that are interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.. We are working towards pushing students in the area of new technologies.


EVENTS AND OUR SESSIONS

BITBOX

BITBOX is GDSC'S annual tech event for students who are interested, all over India. It is a 48-hour long event . The theme of the hackathon is “United Nations 17 SDGs". The first week is reserved for exhilarating and adrenalin pumping 48-hour long Hackathon and mini - events.

30 DAYS OF GOOGLE CLOUD

30 Days of Google Cloud program will provide you an opportunity to kickstart your career in the cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud Platform - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail, and YouTube. Along the way, you will learn & practice concepts like computing, application development, big data & machine learning using the cloud . Also you can showcase what you've learned here to your professional network using Google Cloud-hosted quest & skill badges (see below) that you can add to your resume and professional profiles like LinkedIn . And on top of these amazing badges, get a chance to win some really cool Gooogle Cloud goodies .
